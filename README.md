Idea Title:- 

🧠 CampusCare

Smart Digital Mental Health Support Platform for Students

"Because every student deserves mental support, anytime, anywhere."

Idea Description:- 

🚀 Overview

CampusCare is a student-focused digital mental health support platform designed to help students manage stress, emotional pressure, and academic challenges.

College life often brings academic pressure, placement anxiety, burnout, and personal struggles. 

Unfortunately, many students hesitate to seek help due to stigma or limited access to mental health professionals.

CampusCare creates a safe and supportive digital environment where students can express emotions, track their mood, and receive supportive guidance.

The goal is to combine technology and empathy to promote better emotional well-being for students.

🎯 Problem Statement

Many college students struggle with:
Academic pressure
Anxiety and stress
Lack of accessible mental health support
Fear of judgment when sharing emotions
Despite increasing awareness, most campuses still lack easily accessible mental health support systems.

💡 Our Solution
CampusCare provides a technology-enabled emotional support platform where students can:

Track their daily mood
Write personal reflections
Receive AI-supported suggestions
Build healthy emotional awareness habits
The platform acts as a digital companion supporting students throughout their academic journey.

✨ Key Features
📝 Daily Mood Check-in
Students can log their emotional state daily to build awareness of their mental health patterns.

🤖 AI Reflection Support
The system provides supportive insights and suggestions based on student reflections.

📊 Mood Tracking
Students can observe emotional patterns and identify stress triggers.

🔐 Secure Student Dashboard
Each user has a private and secure space for their emotional reflections.

💬 Supportive Digital Environment
Encourages students to express feelings without fear of judgment.

🏗️ System Architecture

CampusCare follows a web-based client–server architecture.
Workflow:

1️⃣ User logs into the platform

2️⃣ Student interacts with platform features

3️⃣ Backend processes user data

4️⃣ Data is stored securely in the database

5️⃣ Dashboard displays emotional insights and suggestions

🗄️ Database

The project uses MySQL for storing and managing data.

Stored data includes:
User login credentials
Daily mood entries
Reflection notes
User activity data

Third-Party Integrations

AI suggestion logic for emotional reflection support

Cloud-based file sharing for demo and pitch videos
