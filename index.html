Mental Health Screening

Choose a clinically validated screening tool below. The results offer a severity assessment but are **NOT a substitute for professional diagnosis.**

PHQ-9 (Depression)

The Patient Health Questionnaire 9 is a powerful tool for screening, diagnosing, and monitoring the severity of depression.

Questions: 9 | Scoring: 0 - 27

Start Depression Test

GAD-7 (Anxiety)

The Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item scale is used to screen for and measure the severity of generalized anxiety disorder.

Questions: 7 | Scoring: 0 - 21

Start Anxiety Test

PHQ-9 Depression Test

Please rate how often you have been bothered by the following problems over the **last 2 weeks**.

1. Little interest or pleasure in doing things

2. Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless

3. Trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much

4. Feeling tired or having little energy

5. Poor appetite or overeating

6. Feeling bad about yourself - or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down

7. Trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television

8. Moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed. Or the opposite—being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual

9. Thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way

GAD-7 Anxiety Test

Please rate how often you have been bothered by the following problems over the last 2 weeks.

1. Feeling nervous, anxious or on edge

2. Not being able to stop or control worrying

3. Worrying too much about different things

4. Trouble relaxing

5. Being so restless that it is hard to sit still

6. Becoming easily annoyed or irritated

7. Feeling afraid as if something awful might happen

Crisis & Emergency Helplines (India)

Vandrevala Foundation

All counsellors are postgraduate in psychology and full-time employees. They offer multilingual support in practically all Indian languages.

Hours: 24x7. Facilities: Tele counselling (with call back), email, chat, webforms, social media, and soon WhatsApp.

Call: 1860 266 2345 Call: 9999 666 555

Sneha Foundation India

Offers unconditional emotional support to those who are depressed, desperate, and suicidal. They are committed to ensuring you don't have to feel alone.

Hours: 24x7 helpline.

Call: 9144 2464 0050

TISS iCall

Provides telephone-based counselling from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences.

Telephone Counselling Hours: Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 10 PM.

Call: 022-25521111

Voice That Cares

A free public helpline offering psychosocial counselling support for mental health matters including anxiety, exam stress, loneliness, and initial support for suicidal tendencies.

Call: 8448 8448 45

Crisis Support

If you are in immediate danger or a crisis situation, please contact one of the following emergency services:

  • Kiran Mental Health Helpline (India): 1800-599-0019
  • AASRA Helpline (India): +91-9820466726
